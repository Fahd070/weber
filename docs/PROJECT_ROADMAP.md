# Weber — Project Roadmap

> This roadmap is derived from the vision, mission, and long-term direction defined in [`PROJECT_CONSTITUTION.md`](./PROJECT_CONSTITUTION.md). It records what has been established as direction. It does **not** invent dates, scopes, or feature lists that have not been approved by the Project Owner — those are explicitly marked as placeholders below.

---

## Vision

Weber exists to help businesses build a professional digital presence that inspires trust, improves credibility, and supports long-term business growth. The website is not the product — trust, professionalism, and confidence are the product.

Long-term, Weber is intended to evolve from a professional web development company into a complete technology ecosystem, while its core identity — **Building Trust Through Technology** — never changes.

## Long-Term Brand Vision

The brand identity must remain stable and recognizable across every future product Weber ships, regardless of how the technology or offering evolves. Customers should recognize Weber the same way whether they are using the website, a course platform, or a future SaaS product.

Core identity elements that are permanent (per [`BRAND_GUIDELINES.md`](./BRAND_GUIDELINES.md)):

- Mission: Building Trust Through Technology.
- Personality: calm, confident, precise, never loud or exaggerated.
- Visual identity: dark-theme minimalism, the official color palette, and the Weber logo as the single source of truth.
- The snake symbolism informs interaction quality (smoothness, precision, adaptiveness) — it is never used as a literal illustration or mascot.
